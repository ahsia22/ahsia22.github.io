Personal Details

I was born in Galveston, Texas in 2000. However, two years later, I moved to Asia due to my parents' international assignments. I spent the next 13 years growing up and moving between countries in Asia until finally returning to the United States for High School in 2015.

I am interested in the areas of computer security, and artificial intelligence, especially in its financial applications, such as algorithmic trading. In my free time, I enjoy running, learning German, and keeping up with the piano. I'm also an avid student of history and make historical models.
